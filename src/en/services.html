Eco-Friendly Delivery – PMH Relay

At PMHCity, we created PMH Relay, a sustainable delivery service that respects both the environment and the city.
Our goal is to deliver products quickly without increasing the carbon footprint.

How does PMH Relay work?

  • Bicycle couriers
  • Pedestrian delivery agents
  • Use of public transportation

This model ensures fast and efficient delivery without relying on polluting vehicles.

Why it matters

  • No additional CO₂ emissions
  • Less urban congestion
  • Reduced noise pollution
  • Cleaner air for everyone

With PMH Relay, every delivery becomes an eco-friendly action for the city.
